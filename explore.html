Explore Colorado

We're so excited that you're coming to beautiful Colorado! There are some things you should probably know:
  • Drink LOTS of water. At 5,280 feet high (1 mile high), there is less oxygen in the air, so your red blood cells work extra hard and need to be replenished with fluids
  • Sunscreen. We're closer to the sun, which means more UV radiation and a faster rate of burning.
  • Party, but a little bit less than usual. We want you to have a great time! But drop a drink off your usual rate. See bullet #1 - less oxygen, harder working red blood cells, alcohol hits harder, faster, and for longer.
  • Children are welcome! The venue is a beautiful ranch with plenty of green grass to run around. BE AWARE that there is a nearby creek, though. The venue requests that children are supervised.
  • Come a few days early! It takes a few days to acclimate to everything we mentioned above. If you can come a few days early, and explore the beautiful things our state has to offer, we highly recommend it!
